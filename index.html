Trent Building

The Clock Tower is the most iconic landmark of UNNC，was first built in 2004

it adopts classic British architectural style, replicated from its UK counterpart. Standing by the central lake, the grand clock tower strikes every hour.

It serves as the visual axis of the campus, and is also a popular landmark for daily visits and graduation photos.
